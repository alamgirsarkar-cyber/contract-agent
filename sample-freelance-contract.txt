("Contractor")

WHEREAS, the Client desires to engage the Contractor to provide certain services, and the Contractor agrees to provide such services, on the terms and conditions set forth below.

NOW, THEREFORE, in consideration of the mutual promises and covenants contained herein, the parties agree as follows:

1. SERVICES
The Contractor agrees to provide the following services to the Client:
   [DESCRIPTION_OF_SERVICES]

The services shall be performed in a professional and workmanlike manner in accordance with industry standards.

2. TERM
This Agreement shall commence on [START_DATE] and shall continue until [END_DATE], unless earlier terminated in accordance with this Agreement.

3. COMPENSATION
   a) Rate: The Client shall pay the Contractor at the rate of $[RATE] per [hour/project/milestone].
   b) Total Amount: The total compensation shall not exceed $[MAXIMUM_AMOUNT] unless agreed in writing.
   c) Payment Terms: Payment shall be made within [NUMBER] days of receipt of invoice.
   d) Expenses: [Reimbursable/Not reimbursable] expenses must be pre-approved in writing.

4. INVOICING
The Contractor shall submit invoices [frequency] detailing:
   a) Services performed
   b) Hours worked (if hourly)
   c) Expenses incurred (if applicable)
   d) Total amount due

5. INDEPENDENT CONTRACTOR STATUS
The Contractor is an independent contractor and not an employee of the Client. The Contractor:
   a) Is responsible for all taxes, insurance, and benefits
   b) Shall not be entitled to employee benefits
   c) Has the right to control the manner and means of performing services
   d) May work for other clients
   e) Is responsible for providing own equipment and tools

6. WORK SCHEDULE
The Contractor shall determine their own work schedule and methods, provided that:
   a) Deadlines specified by the Client are met
   b) The Contractor is available for meetings and communications as reasonably required
   c) Quality standards are maintained

7. DELIVERABLES
The Contractor shall provide the following deliverables:
   [LIST_OF_DELIVERABLES]

Delivery Schedule:
   - [MILESTONE_1]: [DATE]
   - [MILESTONE_2]: [DATE]
   - [FINAL_DELIVERY]: [DATE]

8. INTELLECTUAL PROPERTY
   a) Work Product: All work product created by the Contractor under this Agreement shall be considered "work made for hire" and shall be the sole property of the Client.
   b) Assignment: To the extent any work product is not considered work made for hire, the Contractor hereby assigns all rights, title, and interest to the Client.
   c) Pre-existing Materials: The Contractor retains ownership of any pre-existing materials incorporated into the work product, but grants the Client a perpetual license to use such materials.

9. CONFIDENTIALITY
The Contractor agrees to:
   a) Keep all Client information confidential
   b) Not disclose confidential information to third parties
   c) Use confidential information only for purposes of this Agreement
   d) Return all confidential materials upon termination

10. NON-SOLICITATION
During the term of this Agreement and for [DURATION] thereafter, the Contractor shall not:
   a) Solicit or attempt to hire Client employees
   b) Solicit Client customers or clients
   c) Interfere with Client business relationships

11. WARRANTIES AND REPRESENTATIONS
The Contractor warrants that:
   a) Services will be performed in a professional manner
   b) Work product will be original and not infringe on third-party rights
   c) Contractor has the right and ability to enter into this Agreement
   d) Work product will be free from defects

12. INDEMNIFICATION
The Contractor agrees to indemnify and hold harmless the Client from any claims, damages, or expenses arising from:
   a) Breach of this Agreement
   b) Negligence or willful misconduct
   c) Infringement of intellectual property rights
   d) Violation of applicable laws

13. LIMITATION OF LIABILITY
Neither party shall be liable for indirect, incidental, or consequential damages. Total liability shall not exceed the total compensation paid under this Agreement.

14. TERMINATION
   a) By Either Party: Either party may terminate this Agreement with [NOTICE_PERIOD] written notice.
   b) For Cause: Either party may terminate immediately for material breach.
   c) Payment Upon Termination: Client shall pay for all services performed up to the termination date.
   d) Return of Materials: Contractor shall return all Client materials upon termination.

15. INSURANCE
The Contractor shall maintain adequate insurance coverage, including:
   a) Professional liability insurance
   b) General liability insurance
   c) Other insurance as reasonably required

16. NO EXCLUSIVITY
This is a non-exclusive arrangement. The Contractor is free to provide services to other clients.

17. AMENDMENTS
This Agreement may only be amended by written instrument signed by both parties.

18. DISPUTE RESOLUTION
   a) Mediation: Disputes shall first be submitted to mediation.
   b) Arbitration: If mediation fails, disputes shall be resolved through binding arbitration.
   c) Governing Law: This Agreement shall be governed by the laws of [STATE/COUNTRY].

19. ENTIRE AGREEMENT
This Agreement constitutes the entire agreement between the parties and supersedes all prior agreements and understandings.

20. SEVERABILITY
If any provision is found unenforceable, the remaining provisions shall remain in full force and effect.

21. ASSIGNMENT
Neither party may assign this Agreement without the written consent of the other party.

22. NOTICES
All notices shall be in writing and sent to the addresses set forth above.
